# Privacy Policy for Klimacek

**Last Updated: November 1, 2025**

## Introduction

Klimacek ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Klimacek (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Location Data

Our App collects and uses your device's location information to provide weather and environmental monitoring services. Specifically:

- **Precise Location (GPS)**: We collect your exact GPS coordinates to provide accurate local climate and weather data
- **Approximate Location (Network-based)**: We may use network-based location to provide regional climate information

**Purpose**: Location data is used to:
- Display local weather conditions
- Show relevant environmental sensor data for your area
- Provide location-specific agricultural recommendations

**User Control**: You can grant or deny location permissions through your device settings at any time.

### 2. Device Information

We automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

- Device model and manufacturer
- Operating system version (Android)
- Unique device identifiers
- Mobile network information
- App version

**Purpose**: This information helps us:
- Ensure app compatibility
- Troubleshoot technical issues
- Improve app performance
- Provide technical support

### 3. Sensor and Environmental Data

The App connects to IoT sensors and collects environmental data including:

- Temperature readings
- Humidity levels
- Light intensity
- Rainfall measurements
- Wind speed
- Solar panel metrics (voltage, current, power)

**Purpose**: This data is used to:
- Display real-time environmental conditions
- Generate charts and analytics
- Provide agricultural insights
- Monitor climate patterns

### 4. Usage Information

We collect information about how you interact with the App:

- Features accessed
- Pages viewed
- Time spent in the App
- Interaction patterns

**Purpose**: To improve user experience and app functionality.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Provide Services**: To deliver core app functionality including weather monitoring, sensor data visualization, and agricultural information
2. **Improve the App**: To understand usage patterns and enhance features
3. **Technical Support**: To troubleshoot issues and provide customer support
4. **Communication**: To send important updates about the App (if you opt-in)
5. **Analytics**: To analyze app performance and user engagement
6. **Compliance**: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage

- Location data is processed in real-time and may be temporarily cached on your device
- Sensor data is transmitted to our secure servers for display and analytics
- We retain data only as long as necessary to provide our services

### Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

- Data encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Secure server infrastructure
- Regular security assessments
- Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
